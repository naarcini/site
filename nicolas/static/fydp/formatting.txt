Map format:
    x-pos:y-pos:C|B|U
        - C = clear
        - B = blocked
        - U = unexplored

    entries in the graph are separated horizontally by commas and vertically with new lines
    map is to be dynamically generated

Instruction format:
    x-pos,y-pos

    These don't have to exist on the map yet to be targeted
    Robot will navigate through unmapped territory and build map along the way
